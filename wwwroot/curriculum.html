Curriculum Structure (B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering)

Total Credits Required: 140

Major Courses: 90 Credits | Minor Courses: 30 Credits | General Education: 20 Credits

This page outlines the complete course structure for your program. Please refer to the official university catalog for the most up-to-date information.

Year 1 - Semester 1

  • Introduction to Computer Science (CSI 101) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: None
  • Calculus I (MTH 101) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: None
  • Physics I (PHY 101) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: None
  • English I (ENG 101) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: None

Year 2 - Semester 1

  • Object-Oriented Programming I (CSI 211) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 101
  • Discrete Mathematics (MTH 201) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: MTH 101
  • Digital Logic Design (ETE 201) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: PHY 101
  • Basic Electronics (ETE 202) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: PHY 101

Year 3 - Semester 1

  • Data Structures (CSI 310) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 211
  • Algorithms (CSI 320) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 310, MTH 201
  • Database Management Systems (CSI 330) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 211
  • Operating Systems (CSI 340) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 211

Year 4 - Semester 1

  • Software Engineering (CSI 410) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 310, CSI 330
  • Artificial Intelligence (CSI 420) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: CSI 320
  • Computer Networks (CSI 430) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: ETE 201
  • Project/Thesis (CSI 499) Credits: 3.0 Prerequisites: 90+ Credits Completed
